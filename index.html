Hello!

I don't know why you're here, but I'm happy you are.

I hot-glued this website together to humble brag about some things, but also—hopefully—to show off some hobbies, failures, and combinations of the two. In whatever I'm doing, I greatly value breadth over depth; I believe interdisciplinary endeavors teach the greatest lessons and provide the best opportunities for meaningful impact in the lives of others. The nice part about this platitudinal-hot-take is that it makes me feel okay admitting I never plan on being done learning. As long as I'm a student of something, I know I'll be happy. This website's here to showcase some of my progress. Enjoy your stay!

A Few Accomplishments

Some professional things, some things I'm just proud of.
